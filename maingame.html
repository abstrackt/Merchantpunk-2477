Gaia

The iron mine of the United Earth Alliance.

Initially discovered during a time of corporate conflict, iron-abundant world of Gaia was quickly colonized and converted into a heavy industry hub to support UEA's war efforts in the Sol System.

After wartime colonization restrictions were lifted civilian population began to flourish. Albeit distant from the home system, Gaia provided near limitless workplaces and was located in a tactical position, neighbouring major warp corridors. Multiple prominent corporations located their transport hubs on Gaia.

Terraforming was considered in the early stages, but due to a lack of major ice caps in the polar regions and a primarily carbon monoxide, low pressure atmosphere the plans were promptly abandoned in favor of self-sustaining biodomes.
